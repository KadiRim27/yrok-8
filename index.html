sadness

Sadness is a negatively colored emotion. Occurs in the case of significant dissatisfaction with a person in any aspects of his life. The concept of sadness is considered to be the opposite of joy and is close in meaning to words such as sadness, longing, despondency, sorrow, kruchina, melancholy. Sometimes these words are considered synonyms.

In the clinical state, sadness turns into depressive states of the body. This can lead to constant bouts of melancholy, bad mood, inability to do ordinary everyday things.

How to overcome sadness

Each of us has bad days when everything goes awry. But do not let them appear in your life too often, so that they do not affect your mood, as well as the mood of your loved ones. When we set ourselves up for something bad, as a result, this bad thing is likely to happen. But remember that all bad things will pass sometime. As they say, time heals.
